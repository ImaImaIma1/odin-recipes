German Chocolate Cake

Description

This is the recipe for creating a German Chocolate Cake.

Ingredients

    Chocolate Cake Ingredients

  • all purpose flour
  • cocoa powder
  • baking powder
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • sugar
  • three eggs
  • vanilla extract

    • German Chocolate Frosting Ingredients

  • brown sugar
  • granulated sugar
  • butter
  • three egg yolks
  • evaporated milk
  • vanilla extract
  • nuts(optional)
  • coconut shavings

Steps

Make the chocolate cake.

  1. Step 1:Combine and mix all the dry cake ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Combine wet cake ingredients.
  3. Step 3: Mix dry and wet mixes together and stir in boiling water and it becomes a rich batter.
  4. Step 4: Preheat and bake at 375Â°F. Grease and flour 8 or 9-inch round baking pans. Pour batter into pan.

Make the German Chocolate frosting.

  1. Step 1: Mix all of the frosting ingredients except for the vanilla, nuts, and coconut into a saucepan.
  2. Step 2: Bring mixture to a low boil on medium heat and stir constantly.
  3. Step 3: Remove from heat and stir in vanilla, nuts and coconut.
  4. Step 4: Cool frosting.
